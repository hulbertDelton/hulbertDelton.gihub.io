Friender Bender

Friender Bender is based off of a game design by Polygon.com's podcast, CoolGames Inc., titled "Sabotage Rally". It is a competitive party racing game, in which you and up to three of your friends take turns driving a single car for a few seconds, trying not to crash while it is your turn. However, it's last man standing, so you need to crash the car while you're not the one driving. Powerups, meta-play, and chaos ensue.
