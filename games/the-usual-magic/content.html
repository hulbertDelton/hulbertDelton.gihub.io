The Usual Magic

The Usual Magic is a game for Virtual Reality, in which you play a young wizard. Cast some happy little spells using gestures on a paint palette-like interface and defeat your foes!

  • Release: December 2016
  • Team Size: 7
  • Project length: 4 months
  • Roles: Concept, design, programming, level design, sound design, production
  • Resources: HTC Vive, Unity, Photoshop

This was a fascinating project for a lot of reasons, both in the work I was doing and in the people I was doing it with. I was effectively in charge of everyone, which was a lot of responsibility, and for that reason I feel a strong sense of ownership over this game. That said, I did not have a hand in the art, and I only managed the tasks and personel issues from the art team. I was the sole engineer on the team, and I think I did a pretty excellent job at it.
