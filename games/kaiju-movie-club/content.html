Kaiju Movie Club

Kaiju Movie Club (tentative title) is a sandbox action game in which you play a teen kaiju, fresh out of the ocean, who just wants to skate. Find a car and pull some sick stunts in the city, knocking down as much stuff as you can in the process!

  • Winter/Spring 2017
  • Team Size: 9
  • Project length: 4 months
  • Roles: 3D art, art lead, technical art
  • Resources: Maya, Unity, Photoshop

Kaiju Movie Club is the game that my team and I, Good Good Games Studio, is making with Dejobaan Games's Ichiro Lambe as a professional adviser. More details to come as the project unfolds.
