Sonic Energy Navigation to Defend, Nurture, and Unite Distant Extraterrestrial Species

This game was made on a whim one Friday to blow off steam. It is an audio-controlled shoot-em-up that quits itself when you win. You can download it here, though I may encourage you to... not... do that.

  • April 29, 2017
  • Team Size: 5
  • Project length: 6 hours
  • Roles: Design, 3D art, technical art, acting (video)
  • Resources: Maya, Photoshop, Unity

I haven't screamed so loudly or laughed so hard before or since the development of this game. It's in my portfolio becuase it brings me joy, not because I think it's a particularly ingenious or well-crafted game.
